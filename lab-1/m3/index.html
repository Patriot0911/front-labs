How to Start Leather Crafting: A Step-by-Step Guide

1. Choose Your Leather

Select the type of leather that suits your project, from full-grain to suede.

2. Gather Your Tools

Make sure you have the essential tools like cutters, stitching awls, and needles.

3. Measure and Cut

Carefully measure and cut the leather according to your design plan.

4. Stitch and Assemble

Use durable threads to stitch your pieces together, ensuring long-lasting strength.

5. Finishing Touches

Polish and seal the leather for a professional finish that will last for years.
